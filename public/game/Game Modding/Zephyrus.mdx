# Zephyrus
`Date Finished: Aug 26 2021`

*An AirBending addon plugin for [ProjectKorra](https://projectkorra.com), and also my second public work with 3941 downloads!*

Uploaded here [https://projectkorra.com/forum/resources/zephyrus.450/](https://projectkorra.com/forum/resources/zephyrus.450/)

Provides an Immersive set of airbending abilities.

## Abilities:

- AirStrike - https://imgur.com/a/ocyOzjB#5RnGqfd
>
> Shoot powerful blasts from all four limbs


- AirBall - https://imgur.com/a/VBflFAA#awaIlXZ
>
> An airbender can create a compressed ball of air by moving their hands together in a circular motion.


- AirPropulsion [Combo] - https://imgur.com/a/SN1adOY#NG3nbQp
>
> Fire yourself forward momentarily..


- SpiritualProjection - https://imgur.com/a/T0LTp6j#4t9LwW3
>
> Master airbenders who have a strong connection with their spiritual side are capable of projecting their spirits into other locations.
