# Dragon
`Date Finished: Dec 28 2021`

*A FireBending addon plugin for [ProjectKorra](https://projectkorra.com) with 3736 downloads!*

Uploaded here [https://projectkorra.com/forum/resources/dragon.456/](https://projectkorra.com/forum/resources/dragon.456/)

Provides an Immersive set of Fire themed abilities.

## Abilities:


- DragonsBreath: https://imgur.com/1q9Ky8o
>
> Phoenix King Ozai showed us a breath which rivalled even that of the dragons. Scorch the earth.

- DragonsComet: https://imgur.com/Ji5iH5G
>
> The Dragon of the West, General Iroh obliterated the mighty walls of Ba Sing Se using DragonsComet.


- DragonsJet: https://imgur.com/zvpX7DR
>
> Phoenix King Ozai demonstrated the power of DragonsJet during Sozin's Comet against Avatar Aang.


- DragonsScales: https://imgur.com/VaTbkAK
>
> Prince Zuko used this to easily counter Princess Azula's deadly strike in an Agni Kai.

- DragonsBolt: https://imgur.com/K2lmDxma
>
> Fire Lord Ozai was shown to be able to generate a powerful lightning strike with great speed during Sozin's Comet.
