# Vulcan
`Date Finished: Aug 8 2021`

*A LavaBending addon plugin for [ProjectKorra](https://projectkorra.com), and also my first public work with 3796 downloads!*

Uploaded here [https://projectkorra.com/forum/resources/vulcan.443/](https://projectkorra.com/forum/resources/vulcan.443/)

Provides an Immersive set of Lava themed abilities.

## Abilities:

- IgneousShift - Hold shift at lava to solidify it or click an earthbendable block to melt it. Alternatively use the slot to negate damage from lava and walk on magma blocks.
>
> Melting - https://imgur.com/VKGFVIp
>
> Cooling - https://imgur.com/a/DMQ6Axd#ShIW2Ot

- Erupt - https://imgur.com/a/3LWXDWR#Blxffjz
>
> Tap shift on an earthbendable block, click to spew magma from the eruption.


- LavaTendril - https://imgur.com/a/FHx6O95#SRzveXj
>
> Tap shift at lava to select a source, click to fire. You can have multiple projectiles.


- LavaStream - https://imgur.com/a/AnnRKvJ#0mtvAEh
>
> Hold shift facing lava to create a stream around you, left click to fire.


- LavaLine - https://imgur.com/a/9tX1WUO#sAVxjDV
>
> Tap shift at an earthbendable block, click to fire.

- [Combo] MagmaMelt - https://imgur.com/a/T9CKSMZ#5S9tMlr
>
> Shoot a LavaLine into a wall, and hold down shift with IgneousShift as the LavaLine makes contact.
